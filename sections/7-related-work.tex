\mypara{White box and black box Input Selection}
As an alternative to property-based testing,
functions may be tested by selecting fixed input/output pairs to check in a test suite.
Methods for selecting such pairs have been studied,
and can broadly be split into \textit{white box} and \textit{black box} methods.
